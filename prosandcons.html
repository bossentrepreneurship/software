The Pros And Cons Of Being A Software Engineer


Nothing is perfect in this world, everything has its pros and cons. - Nivendita Sharma

Pros


Job For The Future


  • Software engineers keep up with the digitally advanced world
  • More startup companies are coming, meaning employers will need more software engineers
  • Artificial intelligence will not replace software engineers for a while due to the fact that they cannot


Flexibility

  • You can work from almost anywhere
  • Most companies do not have a certain time where you have to be at the office
  • Little to no interaction with co-workers


Job For "Lazy" People

  • Being lazy will help you find the simplest solutions to problems
  • Being lazy will allow you to write clean and efficient code
    • I choose a lazy person to do a hard job because a lazy person will find an easy way to do it - Bill Gates

Cons


Repetitive


  • Programming in the same language everyday can be repetitive
  • You will most likely be assigned a task rather that be given an opportunity to create
  • All projects will be similar


Competition

  • A lot of people want to be software engineers
  • 8% of China are software engineers
  • You will need to be very good at programming and problem solving to compete with other people


Work Load

  • You will be given an overwhelming amount of work
  • You need to work every day, even after work hours
  • Projects can take several days to finish
